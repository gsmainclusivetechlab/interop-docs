### Creating a Session

1. [**Click** the **New Session** Button](session)
2. [**Select** the System Under Test (**SUT**) and add its **URL**](session#step-1-sut-selection)
3. [**Add** the **Session Information**](session#step-2-session-setup)
4. [**Choose** the **Test Cases** for the session](session#step-2-session-setup)
5. [**Configure** the SUT](session#step-3-configure-sut)
6. [**Check out** the **Created Session**](session#)
7. [**Send** a **Test Request** to the Interoperability Test Platform](session#running-tests)

### Test Results

1. [**Open** the **Test Session** in the Interoperability Test Platform](results#session-results)
2. [**Access** the **Test Case** of your choice](results#accessing-test-case-results)
3. [**Check** the **Test Information**](results#test-information)
4. [**Analyse** the **Test Run** Information to see it pass or fail and why](results#test-runs)
5. [**Check** the **Test Steps** to see all performed tests for each step](results#test-steps)
6. [**Access** the **Test Flow** to see a sequence diagram with the messages between the steps](results#test-flow)
